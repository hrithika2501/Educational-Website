Grades

KinderGarten

Kindergarten is a preschool educational approach based on playing, singing, practical activities such as drawing, and social interaction as part of the transition from home to school.

1st Standard

Class 1 is the beginners’ stage of learning new things and developing new literacy skills of young learners. In 1st Standard, they take their very first step towards their elementary school education.

2nd Standard

In 2nd grade, kids continue to practice basic arithmetical skills like addition, subtraction, multiplication, etc. and eventually learn to solve mental blocks and add some numbers from their memory.

3rd Standard

In Class 3, students learn about the physical world around them as they tend to make new observations and experiment with new things. In Maths, they tend to learn about different blocks, tiles and shapes.
